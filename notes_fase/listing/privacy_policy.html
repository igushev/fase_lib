Notes Service Privacy Policy

This document describes how Notes Service mobile applications and service ("the Service", "We") collects, uses and shares Your private data.
Protecting and being transparent about Your private data is our priority.
  • Your phone number will be used as Your identifier within the Service;
  • Your name will not be visible to other users;
  • Your information collected within the Service will not be visible to other users;
  • We will do our best to protect Your information;
  • We will not share Your information with any third parties unless required by law.
